product

## Users

Solo individual writer — someone building a daily writing habit who values privacy and simplicity. Could be a content creator who drafts social posts in a private space before publishing to Facebook. The app is open-source, so any individual who wants a focused, encrypted daily note-taking tool can use it. Primary context: a person alone at their desk or on their phone, wanting to capture something quickly without friction.

## Product Purpose

A private, encrypted personal notes app with optional multi-device sync and optional Facebook page publishing. Notes are stored on-device (E2E encrypted). The primary job to be done: open it, write something today, close it. Everything else is infrastructure in service of that loop.

## Brand Personality

Warm. Intimate. Focused. Like a wooden desk with a lamp — not a sterile office, not a cluttered workshop. The app should gently reward the habit of daily writing without being loud about it. Writing comes first; features stay out of the way. Cozy like a journal you carry everywhere.

## Anti-references

- **Notion**: too busy, feature-dense, every surface fighting for attention. The opposite of focused.
- **Google Keep**: card grid chaos, impersonal, feels like sticky notes scattered on a floor.

## Strategic Design Principles

1. The writing surface is the hero. Everything else is infrastructure.
2. Space efficiency means more room for the page, not more visible features.
3. Streak rewards the habit quietly. Whisper, never shout.
4. One action per moment: write now, organize later.
5. Warm but not twee. Cozy but not infantilizing.

## Accessibility

Standard: keyboard navigation, visible focus rings, proper aria labels, respect prefers-reduced-motion.
