Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Notes (notes.ui.pe) is a personal note-taking application developed by The UI Lab. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Notes & media — Your notes and attached images or videos are stored locally on your device using browser storage (IndexedDB). If you enable sync, encrypted copies are transmitted to our sync server.
  • Facebook Page credentials — If you choose to connect a Facebook Page, we store your Page Access Token and Page ID locally on your device (encrypted at rest when a passphrase is set). These credentials are used solely to publish, update, or delete posts on your behalf via the Facebook Graph API.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use cookies for tracking, nor do we embed third-party analytics scripts.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To store and display your notes locally on your device.
  • To sync your encrypted notes across your devices when you opt in.
  • To publish, update, or delete posts on a Facebook Page you have explicitly connected.
  • To display engagement metrics (insights) for your published Facebook Page posts.

4. Facebook Data Usage

When you connect a Facebook Page, the app requests the following permissions:

  • pages_manage_posts — To create, edit, and delete posts on your Page.
  • pages_read_engagement — To fetch post insights such as impressions, reach, reactions, comments, and shares.
  • pages_show_list — To let you select which Page to connect.

We never sell, share, or transfer your Facebook data to any third party. Your Page Access Token is stored only on your device and is transmitted exclusively to Facebook's Graph API endpoints. We do not store any Facebook data on our servers.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All note data is stored in your browser's IndexedDB — it never leaves your device unless you enable sync.
  • Synced data is encrypted end-to-end with a passphrase you control. Our server cannot read your notes.
  • Facebook credentials are stored locally and optionally encrypted with your passphrase.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained only as long as you keep it on your device. You can delete individual notes, clear all data, or disconnect your Facebook Page at any time from within the app. Clearing your browser data will also remove all locally stored information.

To request deletion of any data associated with your Facebook account, you may disconnect your Page from the app's settings or contact us at the email below.

7. Third-Party Services

The app communicates with the following third-party services only when you explicitly use the corresponding feature:

  • Facebook Graph API (graph.facebook.com) — for Page posting and insights.
  • Amazon S3 (*.amazonaws.com) — if you configure S3 backup.

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at privacy@ui.pe.
